Beekeeping
Beekeeping is the activity in charge of raising bees and obtaining the products they generate.
Beekeeping

descripcion
In this sector, honeycombs are stored for the extraction of honey, those who are in charge of this work must wear special clothing for their protection from the bees, they also extract the honey and can avoid bee stings. In addition, not only can honey be extracted from bees, but also jellies, pollen, and other products that we consume today and that can be medicinal for us. There are three types of bees that are: the queen bee responsible for laying eggs, the worker bees that collect nectar and finally the drones which fertilize the queen bee.

Cattle Raising
cattle raising is called the grouping and raising of cows, where products such as milk and many more are extracted from them.
Cattle Raising

descripcion
The cattle ranch has large fields with many hectares where the cows can be fed and can have space so that they are not so stressed, they are also milked and the milk is sold, mainly this milk is sold by colanta and is totally natural without chemical additives.
